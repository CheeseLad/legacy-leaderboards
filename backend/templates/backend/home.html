Legacy Leaderboards
Global leaderboard & achievement tracking for Minecraft Legacy Console Edition
Installation Instructions
Step 1: Download Modified Minecraft
Download Minecraft LCE from the achievement-backend branch:

https://github.com/CheeseLad/MinecraftConsoles
Step 2: Link Your Account
Launch the game and click on "Achievements" in the main menu. This will open the website in your browser where you'll be prompted to create an account and link it to your player UID.
Step 3: View Your Achievements
Once logged in, click on "My Achievements" to view your achievements and their unlock status.
Step 4: Test It Out
Enter a world and open your inventory to unlock the "Open Inventory" achievement. Refresh the achievements page to see it update in real-time.
